# Google Play Store Listing Draft

## App name
Goals_BMI_Calculator

## Short description
Offline BMI calculator and running tracker with history, GPS tracking, charts and dark mode.

## Full description
Goals_BMI_Calculator is an offline-first health and running utility designed to help you calculate BMI and keep track of your personal fitness activity.

### BMI Calculator
• Enter height and weight.
• Calculate BMI instantly.
• View BMI category and visual range.
• Review BMI categories in the BMI chart.

### BMI History
• Save your latest BMI results locally.
• Keep up to 10 recent BMI records.
• Review height, weight, BMI category and date.

### Running Tracker
• Start and stop a running session.
• Use device GPS to estimate distance.
• Track duration and GPS points.
• Save completed runs locally.
• Designed for offline-first use.

### Personalization
• Material-inspired glass UI.
• Blue and white visual theme.
• Light and dark modes.
• Animated rain and water-inspired background.

### Privacy
The app does not require an account. BMI and running records are designed to be stored locally on your device. The running tracker may use GPS while you actively use the feature.

### Important medical disclaimer
This app is for informational purposes only and not a substitute for professional medical advice. BMI is a general screening measure and should not be used as a diagnosis. Consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice.
