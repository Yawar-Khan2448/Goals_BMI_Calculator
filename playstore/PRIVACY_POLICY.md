# Privacy Policy — Goals_BMI_Calculator

**Effective date:** 2026-07-19

Goals_BMI_Calculator is designed as an offline-first health and running utility.

## Information stored on your device
The app may store:
- BMI calculations such as height, weight, BMI result, category, and date.
- Running session information such as distance, duration, date, and GPS route points when you start a run.

This information is stored locally on your device for app functionality. The app does not require an account.

## Location
The running tracker can use your device's location/GPS while you actively use the running tracker to measure distance and record a route. Location access is optional and is only needed for the running tracking feature.

The app is designed to record running data locally. It does not intentionally upload your route to a developer-operated server.

## Internet
The core BMI calculator and locally stored history do not require an internet connection. The app does not require an account or online service to calculate BMI.

## Data sharing
We do not intentionally sell personal information. We do not intentionally share locally stored BMI or running records with third parties.

## Data deletion
You can remove locally stored app data by using your device's app settings to clear the application's storage/data or by uninstalling the application.

## Children's privacy
The app is not specifically directed at children. Parents or guardians should supervise use by minors where appropriate.

## Medical information
The app provides general informational calculations only. It is not a medical diagnostic tool and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

## Changes
This policy may be updated when the app's data practices change.

## Contact
For privacy questions, contact the developer through the contact details provided on the app's Google Play listing.
